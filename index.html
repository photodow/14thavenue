It is far too difficult for anyone to be a disciple of Jesus Christ by themselves. As a congregation, our goal is to encourage every member to build a relationship with God by listening to the words He has spoken in the Scriptures.

What we believe.

We believe that Jesus Christ is the son of God, and that he came down to this Earth as a human sacrifice for the remission of our sins.

Meet our family.

14th Avenue Church of Christ is a small congregation located in the heart of St. Petersburg, FL. We meet every Sunday and Thursday to worship God and study his word. With the birth of 3 new babies and various other new members, our family is growing at a steady pace.

Meet the preacher.

Wayne Welsh and his wife Jenna started working with the 14th Avenue Church of Christ in 2010 and are a huge asset to our family. In the short time they have worked with the congregation, Wayne and Jenna have had 2 children, John and Leah, and Wayne has returned to college to work on his Masters degree in Biblical Studies.
